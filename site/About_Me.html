About Me

 
 
 
 
 

I'm Corbin Davenport (he/him) and I'm a tech writer, software developer, and podcaster living in Raleigh, North Carolina. I currently work for How-To Geek as News Editor, host the Tech Tales podcast, and run the Spacebar blog and newsletter. I also maintain over a dozen open-source web apps, browser extensions, and utilities.


I was formerly a writer at XDA Developers, PC Gamer, and Android Police. I also made a Minecraft blog years ago called Powered by Redstone and Redstone Central.
